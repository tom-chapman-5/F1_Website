The Pinnacle of Motorsport

Formula 1 is known as the pinnacle of motorsports and combines revelutionary engineering and highly skilled drivers to push the cars to the edge. Some of the greatest drivers like Lewis Hamilton, Michael Schumacher and Ayrton Senna has transended the sport and are widly know around the world. Many people are drawn to it because of the high speed, insane crashes and thrilling battles on iconic tracks such as Monaco, Monza, Silverstone and many more. It is also is one of the oldest racing series and is full of history and rivalries.
