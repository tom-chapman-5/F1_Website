Lawrence’s Legion

Aston Martin was a rebranding of Racing point in 2021. Before it was Racing Point it was Force India but after a scandal involing the owener Lawrence Stroll bought the team and it became Racing Point. Stroll is still chairman of the team noe and his son, Lance Stroll also drives for the team alongside 2 time world champion Fernando Aonso.
