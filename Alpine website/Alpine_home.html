The French Team

Alpine is know to be the French team in the sport due to it being a french brand and its previous name being Renault. For the 2023 and 2024 seasons they had an all french line-up with Pierre Gasly and Esteban Ocon. The team, as Alpine, has never won a championship but they have had good moments like Brazil 2024 where they scored a double podium.
