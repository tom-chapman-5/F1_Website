The RedBull Junior Team

Racing Bulls has gone by a few different names initially Toro Rosso then AlphaTauri and now Racing Bulls but they have always been RedBull's junior team but that doesn't mean they haven't had thier own success. At the 2008 Italian Grand Prix Sebastian Vettel won making them the first Red Bull team to win a race. They won their second race in 2020 as AlphaTauri with Pierre Gasly at the Italian Grand Prix.
